## Brand Concept
VESSEL is a tool brand for serious founders - the ones who understand that a token launch is a cultural event, not just a technical one. The name suggests something that holds, carries, and delivers - a container for your community's beliefs, story, and momentum. It's not flashy. It's dependable.

## Color Palette

| Role | Color | Hex |
|------|-------|-----|
| Primary | Ink Black | `#0D0D0D` |
| Accent | Warm Slate | `#4A5568` |
| Highlight | Brass Gold | `#B7922A` |

**Usage notes:**
- Ink Black dominates. It signals seriousness, weight, permanence.
- Warm Slate is for secondary text, dividers, metadata.
- Brass Gold is sparingly applied - pull quotes, CTA borders, section headers. It reads like something worth paying attention to.

---

## Typography Direction

**Primary:** Serif for headlines - something with authority. Think **Playfair Display** or **Freight Display**. These feel like a founder's journal, not a startup pitch deck.

**Secondary:** Clean sans-serif for body copy - **Inter** or **DM Sans** at regular weight. Readable, efficient, no ego.

**Rule:** Headlines are heavy and deliberate. Body copy is invisible - it just carries information.

---

## Tone of Voice

- **Confident, not arrogant.** VESSEL assumes you're smart. It doesn't explain things twice.
- **Sparse.** No filler sentences. If a word doesn't earn its place, cut it.
- **Founder-to-founder.** Written like advice from someone who already launched something - blunt, practical, occasionally dark about the hard parts.
- **Never hype.** VESSEL doesn't promise easy wins. It prepares you for real ones.

**Example of on-brand copy:**
> "Most token launches die before TGE. Not from bad tech - from bad culture. This playbook assumes you're building something worth believing in. The rest is your job."

## Brand Applications

- **Notion template:** Dark mode first. Clean hierarchy. Minimal icons - checkboxes, toggles, dividers only.
- **PDF:** Black background, brass gold headers, serif display type for section titles.
- **Listing copy:** Direct, no emojis, honest about what the product is and isn't.
