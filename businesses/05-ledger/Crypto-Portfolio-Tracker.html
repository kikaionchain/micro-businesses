Setup Guide
Configure once. Use forever.
01
Build Your 5 Databases
Create databases in your note-taking tool of choice (Notion, Obsidian, Airtable, or any spreadsheet). Follow the property definitions exactly. Formulas are provided ‚Äî copy them as-is.
02
Configure Your Settings Page
Create a Settings page. Fill in: your name/handle, portfolio start date, portfolio target (e.g., "Reach $100K by EOY"), your personal trading rules (5-10 rules you commit to), your risk tolerance.
03
Enter Your Current Positions
Enter every position in your Portfolio database. For each: fill every property. For Thesis ‚Äî write it now even if you can't remember why you bought it. The honesty of trying will surface insight.
04
Log Historical Trades
For each position, log at minimum the original buy as a trade in your Trade Log. This gives you a complete history going forward. No need to reconstruct everything from memory.
05
Set Up Your Dashboard
Create a Dashboard page. Add linked views: Active Holdings, Recent Trades (last 30 days), Hot Watchlist, and a Portfolio Summary block with Total Value, P&L, and Position Count. Bookmark it. This is your home.
06
Set Your Weekly Reminder
Every Sunday: Update current prices. Log any trades from the week. First of every month: Complete the Monthly Review. This system only works if you maintain it. 15 minutes weekly, 45 minutes monthly.
